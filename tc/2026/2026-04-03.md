## 1. Q1 Recap & Q2 Priorities

- **Topic:** Q1 2026 Achievements
  - **Discussion:** Amit Handa recapped the successful delivery of the full-funnel UCP roadmap in Q1, including card, checkout, and coupons. Significant progress was made on spec stability, error handling, and maintenance protocols.
  - **Decision:** The team celebrated the progress and diversity of the group's contributions.

- **Topic:** Q2 2026 Priorities & Milestones
  - **Discussion:** Initial priorities for Q2 include:
    - **Features:** Identity, loyalty, local, upsell/recommendations, attributions, and payment enhancements.
    - **Protocol Hardening:** Negotiation, discovery, and version handling.
    - **Tooling:** Improving SDKs, examples, and documentation to lower the barrier for merchants.
    - **Expansion:** Extending the protocol to support other verticals beyond shopping as first-class citizens.
    - **Additions:** Ilya requested adding the **Orders API** (Get Order) to the Q2 roadmap. Amit added **Returns** and enhanced **Analytics/Signals** for better instrumentation.
  - **Topic:** Community Engagement
    - **Discussion:** Ilya noted a growing list of community PRs and issues. Many have the right intent but wrong implementation (e.g., self-hosting vs. core contribution).
    - **Decision:** The group will prioritize triaging these items in Q2 and creating "playbooks" or design principles to guide community contributions.
